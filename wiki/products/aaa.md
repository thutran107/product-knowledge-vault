## What it does
AAA (Advisor Advantage) is Anduin's GP-side interface for RIAs (Registered Investment Advisors) and advisor entities. It enables GPs to manage advisor relationships and subscription workflows in the context of FundSub — including assigning and unassigning subscriptions to advisor entities.

## Key capabilities
- **Assign/unassign subscriptions to advisor entities**: GPs can assign or remove subscriptions from advisor entities within AAA (released Oct 2025).
- Used by Cerity Partners as the GP-side interface ("Advisor Advantage") in the Schwab DocuSign signature workflow.
