## Overview
Anduin's centralized investor data management product. Serves as a firm-level repository for investment entities, contacts, documents, and investor profile data. Enables reusable investor profiles across multiple subscriptions.

## Key Facts
- A "firm" in IDM is an Anduin Object.
- Allows investors to create reusable profiles — eliminating re-entry of the same information for each new subscription.
- Integrates with Salesforce to improve subscription initiation and tracking.
- Reduces duplicative data entry and improves cross-team coordination.
- IDM exposes Settings > Integrations for permission grants (same pattern as FundSub).
- Used by Neuberger Berman to establish a "golden source of truth" for investor data.

## Role in This Knowledge Base
The product that solves the fragmented investor data problem. Referenced primarily in the Neuberger Berman case study and the Configuration vs Permission architecture docs.
