## Overview
InvestorFlow is a standalone investor portal and CRM platform for private markets fund managers. It sits in the same "Standalone" category as Anduin, competing directly on investor relations, document distribution, and reporting.

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Anduin wins:**
- End-to-end platform (FundSub onboarding + IDM + Data Room + Portal) vs. InvestorFlow's narrower scope
- More economical at Enterprise/Whale tiers based on available intel
- Existing Anduin FundSub/IDM customers have low-friction upgrade path to Portal

**Where InvestorFlow is strong:**
- Established market presence in the standalone portal category
- AUM-based pricing may be simpler for customers to model at smaller scales

## Pricing intel (as of May 2025)
- Source: ex-employee
- Pricing structure: based on AUM
- $20–30K/yr for $1B AUM GPs
- $100–150K/yr for $10B AUM GPs

**Anduin comparison at same tiers:**
- Enterprise ($1B–15B): $15K/fund/yr — competitive vs. InvestorFlow
- Whale tiers: $20–30K/fund/yr — comparable or better depending on fund count

## Known objections & responses
- **"InvestorFlow has an established track record"** — Anduin's portal is built on a proven platform (FundSub, IDM, Data Room already deployed); integration is native, not bolted on.
- **"InvestorFlow is already in our stack"** — Anduin's full bundle (FundSub + IDM + Portal) may replace multiple point solutions at lower total cost.
