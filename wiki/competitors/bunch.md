## Overview
bunch is a German (DACH-region) full-suite private-markets platform — onboarding, LP portal, fund accounting, fund-admin services, portfolio monitoring, and treasury. Founded ~2021, ~90 people (~80% in Germany), Motive Partners-backed. Raised a **$35M Series B** (Portage, Illuminate Financial, Motive Partners) and is **expanding into the UK** beyond its DACH base.

## How they compare to Anduin
- **Broad suite, regionally concentrated** — historically DACH-only; UK expansion means it will start appearing on **portal deals** outside Germany more often.
- Competes most directly with **Vestlane** and **AtomInvest** in Europe.

## Known objections & responses
- **"bunch covers onboarding through fund accounting"** — note regional concentration; for international/complex GPs, position Anduin's depth and end-to-end integration. Watch UK pipeline.
