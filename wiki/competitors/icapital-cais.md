## Overview
iCapital (often grouped with CAIS) is the dominant **wealth-channel alternatives distribution** platform — feeder funds and access for advisors/RIAs into private markets. It overlaps Anduin most on the **wealth/RIA onboarding** surface and increasingly on **reusable investor identity** after acquiring Parallel Markets.

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where iCapital is strong:**
- Massive advisor/wealth distribution network and feeder-fund infrastructure.
- **iCapital Investor Passport** (from the Parallel Markets acquisition) — reusable investor passport + AI/ML-powered end-to-end onboarding, directly overlapping Anduin [[wiki/products/investor-data-management|IDM]] and [[wiki/products/fundsub]] on the wealth channel.

**Where iCapital is weak / Anduin's opportunity:**
- **No direct communication with the fund**; pre-review submission workflows; **cannot pre-fill by entity type** (an Anduin strength — IA/autofill prefill by entity type, then investor-specific prefill).
- AUM growth slowed in the trailing 12 months as advisors create and manage **their own co-invest vehicles** to avoid iCapital fees — a structural opening for Anduin.

## Strategy notes
- Acquired **Parallel Markets** (Feb 2025/2026 announcements) for reusable investor-passport tech; plan to add automated entry for alternative investing and a complete AI/ML onboarding solution.

## Known objections & responses
- **"We use iCapital for wealth distribution"** — different layer; position Anduin on direct GP↔LP onboarding, entity-type prefill, and data integrity. The co-invest-vehicle trend is a wedge to sell GPs direct tooling.
