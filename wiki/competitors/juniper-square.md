## Overview
Juniper Square is a private-markets investor-management platform (portal, fund admin, fundsub) strongest among **emerging managers and simpler fund structures**, now investing heavily in AI (**JunieAI**, a $130M Series D) and MCP-based products (**Headless GPX**).

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Anduin wins (Fundsub):**
- Juniper's Fundsub has an **outdated, simple UI**, uses a **universal-questionnaire hybrid** with highly restricted customization and minimal logic rules, **extremely limited workflow/review** (no commenting seen), sub-standard dashboard/reporting, and **limited AML/KYC**.
- Categorizes investors into only 2 buckets (Individuals + Entities); **no Investor Passport**.
- Net: "a good solution for emerging managers or those with very simple docs."

**Where Juniper is strong:**
- **Collects/facilitates payments** through the platform (likely via its fund-admin arm) — a feature Anduin doesn't have.
- Aggressive AI investment: **JunieAI** (enterprise agentic AI for GPs) and **Headless GPX** (MCP product) — a bet that the interface shifts toward AI assistants; a strategic watch item for Anduin's stickiness.

## Known objections & responses
- **"Juniper Square does everything incl. fund admin + payments"** — position Anduin's depth on onboarding workflow, logic, AML/KYC, and investor passport; Juniper fits simpler/emerging managers. Take the AI roadmap (JunieAI/MCP) seriously for retention.
