## Overview
Vega is an **Apollo-backed** alternative-investment workflow platform pursuing an end-to-end "AltsOS" strategy. Apollo contributed its **Edna** investment platform into Vega, and Apollo's **LYRA** fund-onboarding solution is built on Vega. As of 2025 the product is early — its focus is delivering an MVP for Apollo before a broader go-to-market.

## How they compare to Anduin
- **Not yet a fully formed product** (as of early 2025); building for Apollo first, GTM expected later by approaching large players to showcase the Apollo-built platform.
- Pursuing **wide integration with "sister companies"** and partnered with **IQ/EQ for KYC/AML**.
- Retail-distribution focus — as retail-oriented alts products gain traction, expect large firms to ask how Anduin handles distribution to retail investors.

## Known objections & responses
- **"Apollo is building Vega"** — monitor; today it's Apollo-internal/MVP, not a broad market product. Watch for 2026 GTM. Related: [[wiki/competitors/competitor-landscape-watchlist|LYRA]] runs on Vega.
