## Overview
BiteStream **rebranded to Investor Pointe** (2026) and positions as an **end-to-end Investor Experience platform** (digital onboarding + multi-jurisdiction KYC/AML + portal). It raised **$25M from NewSpring Capital** (Fall 2025), currently serves small GPs, and plans to move **upmarket to enterprise**.

## How they compare to Anduin
- Currently small-GP focused; the watch item is the **move upmarket** into mid-market fundsub and portal deals.
- Hamilton Lane (an Anduin customer) considered BiteStream for its **end-to-end story** (digital onboarding + multi-jurisdiction KYC/AML).
- Internal read is skeptical of execution (Paul: "They will Bite the dust soon.").

## Known objections & responses
- **"BiteStream/Investor Pointe is end-to-end"** — position Anduin's maturity and proof at scale; they're early in the enterprise move.
