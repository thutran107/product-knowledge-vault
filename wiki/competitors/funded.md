## Overview
"Funded" is the **fund-subscription tooling associated with Kirkland & Ellis (K&E)** — a law-firm-built/channel offering. Anduin loses deals when a GP's counsel is K&E and the firm pushes its own template docs and tooling. See [[wiki/competitors/kirkland-ellis]].

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Anduin wins:**
- **Funded doesn't surface sub-doc completion status on a dashboard** the way Anduin does (a concrete gap raised by Flexpoint Ford, ~$10B AUM, exploring alternatives after a bad K&E experience).
- That same Flexpoint conversation opened a [[wiki/products/data-room]] opportunity (they had a poor SS&C Intralinks portal experience getting LPs into the subdoc environment).

**Where Funded/K&E is strong:**
- **Law-firm channel control** — Gen II pushed clients to use K&E template docs instead of prospect subdocs; NEA switched counsel Proskauer→K&E and adopted K&E's product.

## Known objections & responses
- **"Our law firm (K&E) provides the subscription tooling"** — position Anduin's dashboard/completion visibility, workflow depth, and that law-firm tools optimize for the firm, not the GP/LP experience.
