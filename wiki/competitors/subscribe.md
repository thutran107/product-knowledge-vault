## Overview
Subscribe (also "Subscribe+" / SUBSCRIBE platform) competes with Anduin in fund subscription with a strong **managed-services** angle and an increasingly **platform/marketplace** strategy. It bundles managed services and API into a flat platform fee, and in 2025 launched **Altscape**, a no-fee private-fund marketplace connecting 3,500+ GPs to 50,000+ advisors across 600+ wealth-management firms. It has been adopted by large managers (DWS, PGIM, Coller Capital) and is expanding from onboarding toward a broader investor passport + portal + post-trade reporting stack.

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Subscribe is strong:**
- **All-in pricing with managed services included** — flat $45K platform fee bundling per-fund implementation, offline-subscription managed collection/import, API, and a 3-month free trial. This reframes deals around a single low number vs. Anduin's itemized quote.
- **Straightforward LP onboarding flow**; **Manual Subscription Module** (upload offline subdocs, view all submissions on one digital tracker).
- **Marketplace/distribution** (Altscape) and reach into wealth/RIA channels.
- **Universal Investor Passport** (rebranded) for reusable identity / recurring AML/KYC; **EXTRACT** for post-trade reporting; an LP Portal product (revealed in a deal cycle against InvestorFlow).

**Where Anduin wins:**
- **Depth and robustness** — acknowledged by prospects (e.g., Alyssa) as "more robust" and "the market leader"; Subscribe wins on cost and lift, not capability.
- **End-to-end platform integration** and stronger workflow/logic.

## Pricing intel (directional)
- **$45K annual platform fee, all-in**: 6-hr implementation per fund at no extra cost (incl. updates), full managed services (offline collection + manual import), API integration included, 3-month free trial.
- Illustrative head-to-head (EIG-style deal): Subscribe year-1 ~$45K vs. an itemized Anduin quote reaching ~$176K (platform $45K + data import $8K + data room $10K + API $15K + per-fund $8.5K×5 + OCR ~$11.25K/fund). The gap is largely managed-services bundling and itemization — a key competitive-pricing lesson.

## Win/loss
- **Lost to Subscribe:** Adam's Street (lacking API), GTIS (LP Profile + API), HPS (LP Profile), DWS ($84B; redemption experience + streamlined process), EIG (price & time-to-delivery).
- Pattern: losses cluster on **price, API, LP Profile, and managed-services lift** rather than core onboarding UX.

## Strategy notes
- Adopted by Coller Capital (an Anduin customer) to broaden alternative-investment access; PGIM has funds on the platform.
- Altscape marketplace launch prompted internal discussion on whether Anduin should build its own marketplace given existing GP relationships.

## Known objections & responses
- **"Subscribe is $45K all-in with managed services"** — unpack the itemization gap; Anduin's depth, data integrity, and platform breadth justify premium, but be ready to bundle and to position managed services. The flat-fee + free-trial motion is Subscribe's strongest lever.
- **"They include API / managed import"** — match on capability framing; emphasize Anduin's robustness and that Subscribe's manual import is process, not integration.
