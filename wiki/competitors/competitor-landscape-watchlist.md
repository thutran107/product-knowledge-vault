## Overview
Compact roundup of **single-mention** competitors and potential competitors from the [[wiki/sources/competitive-intel-tracking-table]]. Each has one logged intel entry — not enough for a standalone page, but worth tracking. Grouped by competitive theme. Promote any entry to its own page when intel accumulates.

> Confidence is **low** for every entry here — each rests on a single observation. Verify before using in a live deal.

## Side-letter / MFN rivals
- **Pro-Vision (PV)** *(potential competitor, Mar 2025)* — perceived as better than [[wiki/competitors/ontra|Ontra]] because it aligns with how law firms work (supports the outsource-to-firm model, cross-client visibility, self-service uploads). Paul Weiss prefers PV's collaborative approach over Ontra's competitive stance.
- **Robin AI** *(potential competitor, Mar 2023)* — Proskauer Rose LLP uses Robin AI for side-letter term tracking; co-creates closely with the firm (similar to Anduin's law-firm relationships).
- **Carta** *(competitor, Sep 2024)* — has a **more developed side-letter solution** than Anduin; a competitor reps even refer side-letter needs to Carta and subscription needs to Anduin. Anduin's side-letter maturity is a known gap here.

## AI-native / LP-side intelligence
- **Vantager** *(potential competitor, Jul 2025)* — AI-native LP-allocator intelligence platform (portfolio monitoring, fund data). Flagged as the LP-side AI competitor to watch; recommendation that Anduin build LP-facing tools to avoid being outflanked.
- **Daphne** *(potential competitor, May 2025)* — AI platform that digitizes/centralizes fund data for alt managers; backed by Apollo + Motive Partners; integrates with Hamilton Lane's Cobalt; automates DDQ responses. RIA use cases.
- **BlueFlame.ai** *(potential competitor, Aug 2025)* — started as a ChatGPT wrapper for investment teams, expanded to agentic AI + IR-team positioning; **acquired by Datasite**. Datasite + BlueFlame = data room + AI IR layer, a combined threat across [[wiki/products/data-room]] and LP communications.

## European / wealth-channel platforms
- **Privatize** *(potential competitor, May 2025)* — German B2B platform connecting wealth managers to private-market funds; founded 2023 by ex-Deutsche/BlackRock team; **Carlyle Wealth Management signed**; custodian integrations; potential iCapital challenger in Europe.
- **Vestlane** *(potential competitor, Jul 2023)* — focus on **LP Transfer**; European; competes with [[wiki/competitors/bunch]] and [[wiki/competitors/atominvest]].
- **iAltA** *(potential competitor, Jun 2025)* — new entrant from the ex-Ipreo team (Ipreo→IHS→S&P); full suite (marketing data room, KYC, e-subscription, portal); raised $20M from WestCap. Parent ecosystem behind **Verivend/Veriform** (below).
- **Verivend / Veriform** *(potential competitor "Veriend", Dec 2025)* — Veriform is Verivend's digital fund-subscription product (conditional logic, e-sign, progress tracking); credible **mid-market** digitization tool selling to GPs; lighter depth per module. Anduin wins where scale/rigor/integration matter.

## Fund-admin / portfolio / data incumbents
- **Arcesium** *(potential competitor, Dec 2024)* — post-trade processing; used by Cerberus; targets hedge funds + institutional managers (portal adjacency).
- **Mantle Portal** *(potential competitor, Dec 2024)* — Canadian startup; cap-table management + Canoe-type LP portal.
- **Dynamo** *(potential competitor, Mar 2026)* — acquired **InvestHub** (~20-person French firm) to bring sub-doc in-house and end reliance on its [[wiki/competitors/passthrough|Passthrough]] integration. Bar is "just good enough"; Dynamo BDRs will push to their base — watch fundsub deals where Dynamo/Allvue is the admin.

## Marketplace / transfers / other
- **Zanbato** *(potential competitor, Jun 2026)* — marketplace for private-market transactions (tender offers, **LP transfers**); expanding into fund accounting; 8VC-backed; growing threat in LP-transfer space Anduin hasn't addressed.
- **LYRA** *(competitor, Mar 2025)* — Apollo's fund-onboarding solution built on **Vega** ([[wiki/competitors/vega]]).
- **Intralinks (InvestorVision)** *(potential competitor, Feb 2026)* — rebranded to InvestorVision; platform refresh to justify price increases on legacy data-room customers; Centerbridge and ICONIQ unhappy ("glorified SharePoint doc distribution"). Active opportunity to pick up displaced [[wiki/products/data-room]] customers.
- **Altruist** *(potential competitor, Apr 2025)* — digital-first custodian for RIAs (portfolio accounting, performance reporting, billing, trade balancing); RIA-channel adjacency.
- **Techquity** *(competitor, Dec 2024)* — Anduin **won** a $29.7K deal over Techquity (CapMan, Nordics).
- **AXA (AXA IM)** *(potential partner, Nov 2024)* — $900B French asset manager; acquired W Capital (an Anduin client) → potential relationship-expansion, not a competitor.
