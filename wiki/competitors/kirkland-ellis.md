## Overview
Kirkland & Ellis (K&E) is a top fund-formation **law firm** that acts as a **channel competitor** to Anduin: it steers its GP clients toward its own subscription tooling and template docs (see [[wiki/competitors/funded]]) and has built **SideTrack**, a side-letter platform for investment-fund attorneys. Tagged variously as "potential partner" and "competitor" in the intel — relevant because Anduin loses deals where K&E is counsel.

## How they compare to Anduin
- **Channel control** — when K&E is the GP's counsel, the firm pushes K&E template docs / its product over Anduin (e.g., Patient Square Capital lost with K&E as the law firm; NEA switched counsel Proskauer→K&E and adopted K&E's product).
- **SideTrack** competes in the side-letter space (cf. [[wiki/competitors/ontra]], Pro-Vision, Carta).
- Law-firm tooling optimizes for the firm's workflow, not necessarily the GP/LP experience — Anduin's wedge (see the dashboard-visibility gap noted under [[wiki/competitors/funded]]).

## Known objections & responses
- **"Our counsel K&E handles the subscription docs"** — position Anduin's GP/LP experience, completion visibility, and platform depth; emphasize that the firm's tooling is a byproduct, not a product investment.
