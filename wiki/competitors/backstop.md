## Overview
Backstop (Backstop Solutions) provides an **investor portal + CRM** used by allocators and managers. In Anduin's intel it appears as an incumbent portal with **poor LP engagement and clunky administration** — a displacement opportunity rather than a strong threat.

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Anduin wins:**
- **Sequoia** used Backstop's client portal and found it **clunky, hard to administer, poor engagement**, with no unique/branded experience.
- **Jasper Ridge** used Backstop for portal + CRM but plans to **build their own** to support complex client types — wanting better reporting, dashboards, and investor access to metrics beyond documents.

## Known objections & responses
- **"We already have Backstop for our portal"** — lead with engagement, branded LP experience, and reporting/dashboard depth; incumbents cite administration pain and weak engagement.
