## Overview
Fenergo is an **enterprise compliance / KYC-AML** software vendor with a large existing institutional client base. In late 2025 / early 2026 it launched a **digital subscription tool alongside an investor portal**, opening a new competitive surface adjacent to Anduin's Fundsub + Portal.

## How they compare to Anduin
- **Horizontal, not purpose-built** — Fenergo's core is broad onboarding/compliance/KYC-AML across general-ledger-, custody-, and account-centric use cases, rather than purpose-built private-markets tooling.
- **Not yet mature** in the new surface — Citadel left a Fenergo demo "very confused about capabilities."
- Threat lever is its **enterprise compliance install base**, not product depth (today).

## Known objections & responses
- **"Fenergo already does our compliance"** — position Anduin's purpose-built private-markets onboarding depth; Fenergo's subscription/portal product is early and horizontal.
