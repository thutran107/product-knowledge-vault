## Overview
Flow is a fund-subscription/onboarding competitor that was **acquired by Apex Group** (a large global fund-services provider) in April 2025. The acquisition gives Flow **enterprise distribution via Apex's existing fund-admin client base** — flagged internally as a significant expansion of Flow's competitive reach.

## How they compare to Anduin
- Pre-acquisition, a Fundsub competitor; post-acquisition, the threat is **distribution scale** through Apex, not standalone product differentiation.
- Another instance of the **fund admin absorbing onboarding** pattern (cf. [[wiki/competitors/nav]], Dynamo→InvestHub).
