## Overview
Passthrough (PT) is Anduin's most frequently encountered competitor in fund subscription (Fundsub), especially among emerging and mid-market GPs. It offers two main products: **digital subscription document onboarding** and **managed AML/KYC compliance**. Its go-to-market is anchored by a strong law-firm channel (notably a Goodwin vendor partnership) and a deliberate low-price-then-cross-sell motion. By PT's own webinar numbers it onboarded ~260 funds in 2024 (~5/week), estimated ~$2.5–3M Fundsub revenue (vs. Anduin's ~$8M).

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Passthrough is strong:**
- **Price** — repeatedly undercuts Anduin, and its **one-time, non-recurring per-fund fee** (good for the life of the fund) is a major sticking point in losses (e.g., NYCA: PT $18K one-time vs. Anduin $61.5K; Centre Lane: $22K one-time).
- **LP user interface** — wins on simplicity; described by Hamilton Lane as "the TurboTax of private markets."
- **In-app AML/KYC + OFAC checking** — ongoing screening throughout the fund lifecycle (not just onboarding), which makes it stickier and has directly cost Anduin deals.
- **Law-firm channel** — Goodwin partnership (serving 40+ Goodwin clients) drives referrals and has even pushed to displace Anduin at renewal (e.g., 2150 VC).
- **Integrations** — out-of-the-box Salesforce, plus Snowflake, Altvia, Dynamo; partnerships with eFront (free SSO), BlackRock Aladdin, Allvue, Entrilia.

**Where Anduin wins:**
- **Platform maturity** — Apollo's pilot found PT "not mature enough"; Anduin won Silver Point despite quoting $30K vs. PT's $15K on maturity.
- **UI + SSO** — won Lattice Capital over PT (PT cheaper) on sleek UI and SSO.
- **Conditional logic / digitization depth** — PT's logic is basic, often asks the same question multiple ways, doesn't digitize every question word-for-word.
- **CS support** — Fin Capital ($1B) left PT citing poor CS, slow response, difficulty making edits; low adoption noted at TA Associates (3%).
- **FIS / fund-admin integration** (Investran, DX) — a key differentiator in some deals.

## Pricing intel (directional — individual deal quotes)
**Fundsub:**
- Emerging fund: ~$5–7.5K (InVivium); $10K + $7K OFAC (an emerging fund, Jan 2023).
- Per-fund general: $7–15K (informal partner, 2023); $15–20K per fundraise (2024, "pulling our price down").
- Enterprise: $30K for Fundsub + 1 fund (2022); $18K one-time for 3 vehicles (NYCA); $22K one-time (Centre Lane); $40K + $7.5K implementation → negotiated to $20K annual (Blue Torch, $7.2B AUM).
- New (2026): managed sub-doc review at **$250/LP**; updated "$11,500 all-in for sub docs + $275/LP for KYC/AML" (Raine, $3B AUM).

**AML/KYC:**
- $75 per entity check (2023); ~$100/yr/investor (2025).

## Product & strategy notes
- **Two-product funnel:** cheap Fundsub to acquire customers, cross-sell downstream into managed AML/KYC (100+ KYC customers by end of 2023). Launched an **AML Compliance Officer (AMLCO)** product (uses ComplyAdvantage + human review) and is building org-level compliance tooling. Streamlining a Cayman KYC managed process (partnership with Leeward).
- **2025 roadmap (per webinar):** data extraction + investor data management (cross-fund tracking, centralizing investor docs) — moving onto Anduin's IDM turf.
- **Launched** Investor Passthrough (investor passport) + Fundsub API.
- Building compliance as a standalone service retainable absent onboarding.

## Known objections & responses
- **"Passthrough is cheaper / one-time fee"** — true on sticker, but Anduin's bundle (FundSub + IDM + Data Room + Portal + Integration Hub) replaces multiple tools; emphasize maturity, CS quality, SSO, and total cost of ownership. PT's non-recurring fee is its single strongest lever — expect it in price-sensitive emerging deals.
- **"Passthrough does our AML/KYC in-app"** — historically Anduin's gap; PT's check satisfies bank requirements and runs ongoing. Position Anduin's AML/KYC and managed-services path; this is a known loss driver to address head-on.
- **"Our law firm (Goodwin) recommends Passthrough"** — channel-driven; counter with maturity, references, and LP experience proof points.

## Security signal
- Willkie Farr had a 2FA incident with a PT-adjacent partner and is evaluating Anduin as a result.
