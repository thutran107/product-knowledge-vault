## Overview
AtomInvest is a standalone investor portal platform for private markets, positioned in the same "Standalone" category as Anduin. It has extended capabilities in onboarding and accounting alongside its portal product.

## How they compare to Anduin
**Where Anduin wins:**
- Better UX and branding customization per the product overview
- End-to-end platform integration (FundSub → IDM → Data Room → Portal) is native; AtomInvest's integrations are more fragmented
- Established Anduin customer base has low-friction upgrade path

**Where AtomInvest is strong:**
- Lower pricing at the Enterprise tier vs. Anduin list price
- Extended capabilities in accounting may appeal to firms that want a broader suite from one standalone vendor
- Established market presence

## Pricing intel (as of May 2025)
- Source: ex-employee
- Pricing structure: per-fund, tiered by AUM
- $10K/fund/yr for $1B+ AUM GPs
- $6K/fund/yr for <$1B AUM GPs

**Anduin comparison:**
- Emerging ($0–250M): $6K/fund/yr — matches AtomInvest
- Established ($250M–1B): $8K/fund/yr — slightly higher than AtomInvest's $6K
- Enterprise ($1B–15B): $15K/fund/yr — higher than AtomInvest's $10K; justified by broader product bundle

Anduin guidance: at Enterprise/Whale, our pricing is higher than AtomInvest but our end-to-end product bundle (FundSub + IDM + Landing Pages + Portal + Integration Hub) is stronger and justifies the premium.

## Known objections & responses
- **"AtomInvest is cheaper at our tier"** — Anduin's full bundle replaces multiple point solutions (fund admin portal + onboarding tool + data room); total cost of ownership comparison favors Anduin when counting all tools replaced.
- **"AtomInvest has accounting features"** — Anduin focuses on the investor relationship layer; customers using Allvue/FIS for accounting can integrate via Integration Hub.
