## Overview
Global, independent investment management firm founded in 1939, headquartered in New York City. Manages $460B+ AUM across equities, fixed income, private equity, and alternatives. Wholly employee-owned. One of Anduin's marquee case study customers.

## Key Facts
- Products deployed: FundSub, Data Room, Investor Access, IDM, Integration Hub.
- Core problem solved: no golden source of truth for investor data; duplication across CRM and fund admin tools.
- Scope: dozens of fundraises annually; closed-ended and evergreen funds; PE, real estate, crypto; multiple jurisdictions (Delaware, Cayman, Luxembourg).
- Key initiative: "Omnibus Version" — a standardized subscription document applied across all funds.
- IDM used for reusable investor profiles; Salesforce integrated for subscription tracking.
- Key contact: Christa Teevan, SVP Client Services.
- Results: ~3,000 LPs onboarded, 90% initiation rate, 78% completion rate within days, $5.4B raised.

## Role in This Knowledge Base
Primary case study demonstrating the full Anduin platform deployed at scale. Validates the IDM + FundSub + Integration Hub combination for large asset managers.
