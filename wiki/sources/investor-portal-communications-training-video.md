## Summary
The Communications workflow gives GPs a dedicated channel for announcements/updates that are **separate from automated document or invitation notifications** — "a direct line for any message that does not necessarily come with a document attached." Covers the compose flow, template library, dynamic placeholders, the Sent Items audit trail, and the draft/review model.

## Key Takeaways
- **Dedicated GP messaging channel**, distinct from system-generated notifications.
- **Sent Items dashboard** logs every outbound investor email (custom message, document share, invitation, document request) with recipient counts, sender, and date; **email-type tags** make it filterable — a compliance/tracking record that survives staff turnover.
- **Reusable template library** at firm level: subject + body default from templates; create/edit/swap without leaving compose. **Manual edits always win** — "the final version sent will always reflect what is shown in the fields at the time of sending."
- **Dynamic placeholders** (receiver full name, firm name, etc.) substitute per recipient at send; the system **flags unsupported placeholders** before delivery.
- **Individual vs. shared toggle:** default individual (privacy, no reply-all); shared sends one email to a group (e.g., an LP's internal team). CC/BCC via the same contact picker.
- **Attachments auto-sync to the Documents dashboard** for both GP and LP — no double upload. CTA button only links pages visible to all selected recipients.
- **Drafts** support collaborative, asynchronous review before sending.
