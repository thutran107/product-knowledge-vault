## Summary
General knowledge deck covering the Investor Portal concept, market context, competitive landscape by category, and the Anduin ecosystem positioning. Shorter companion to the full sales deck and internal primer. Includes the clearest competitive category table of the batch.

## Key Takeaways
- Same core narrative as the primer: portal is a secure digital platform for LP communication, document management, and reporting.
- Competitive category table is particularly useful for positioning conversations.
- MVP investor experience includes: customizable home, vehicle landing pages, Data Room, document library, inbox, tasks. Reporting dashboards and profiles flagged as "not in MVP."
- Acquisition goals stated (same as primer): $14M CFP by end of 2026.

## Competitive Category Table

| Category | Examples | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|---------|-----------|------------|
| Data Room-Based | Intralinks, Datasite, Merrill | Strong security, granular access, doc tracking | Limited customization for fund workflows or reporting |
| Fund Admin-Provided | SS&C, SEI, Ultimus | Integrated with accounting/compliance, cost-effective when bundled | Limited functionality, branding, customization |
| Accounting-Based | Allvue, FIS DX/Investran, eFront | Strong financial analytics, familiar to admins | Overly technical, rigid, poor investor experience |
| Standalone [Anduin] | InvestorFlow, Fundwave, AtomInvest | Highly customizable, user-friendly, strong branding | May need additional integrations with existing systems |
| Custom-Built | Proprietary | Maximum customization | High cost, slow time-to-market |
| Banking/Enterprise | Blackstone iLevel, BNY Mellon NEXEN | Enterprise-grade scalability and security | Generalized, not focused on private markets |

## MVP vs. Not-In-MVP Features
**MVP (live at launch):**
- Customizable home page with section templates and Anduin widgets
- Vehicle landing pages
- Integrated Data Room per vehicle
- Document library (centralized, search, filter, batch download)
- Centralized inbox
- Tasks (auto-created, deadlines, reminders)

**Not in MVP (post-launch):**
- Reporting dashboards
- Profiles (investor self-service for contact/wire updates)
