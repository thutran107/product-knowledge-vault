## Summary
Case study of Neuberger Berman — a $460B AUM global investment firm — adopting Anduin across Fund Subscription, Data Room, Investor Access, IDM, and Integration Hub to eliminate fragmented data, streamline investor onboarding, and raise $5.4B on the platform.

## Key Takeaways
- Core problem: no golden source of truth for investor data; same fields duplicated across CRM, fund admin tools, etc.
- Challenge scope: closed-ended and evergreen funds across PE, real estate, crypto; multiple jurisdictions and AML/KYC requirements; legal counsel from multiple firms.
- Anduin approach: phased — 8 pilot closed-ended forms → 8 more with data standard → open-ended + LP transfers.
- "Omnibus Version": a standardized subscription document applied across all funds for consistency.
- IDM used to create reusable investor profiles — eliminates re-entry per subscription.
- Salesforce integrated via Integration Hub for subscription initiation and tracking.
- Products used: FundSub, Data Room, Investor Access, IDM, Integration Hub.
- Results: ~3,000 LPs onboarded, 90% initiation rate, 78% completion rate within days, $5.4B raised.
