## Summary
Primary Investor Portal sales deck covering the full product vision, investor experience features, fund manager capabilities, financial reporting module, and 2026 roadmap. Most comprehensive capabilities reference for Sales + RM.

## Key Takeaways
- Anduin Investor Portal is a unified investor communication platform spanning pre-marketing through post-close activities.
- Core investor experience: customizable branded home page, vehicle landing pages, integrated Data Room, centralized document library, inbox, tasks, financial reporting dashboards, and investor profile/wire update workflows.
- Core GP experience: self-service page editor, contact matrix management, batch document distribution (group share / private share / split & share), email composition with templates and placeholders, AI-powered document-to-entity mapping.
- Financial Reporting is a 3-layer framework: (1) fund-defined datasets, (2) flexible display widgets, (3) audience-specific reporting pages — all tied to Anduin's permission/contact matrix model.
- Full Anduin platform integration: Portal + IDM + Landing Pages + FundSub + Data Room + Side Letter + Integration Hub as an end-to-end GP–LP relationship platform.
- 2026 roadmap is explicit in this deck (see below).

## Feature Detail

### Investor Experience
- **Customizable home page:** Section templates (hero banner, about us, teams, etc.); Anduin widgets surface pending tasks and recent documents; mobile-friendly.
- **Vehicle landing pages:** Per-fund customizable pages; promote upcoming vehicles to existing LP base; integrated Data Room per vehicle (granular permissions, real-time analytics).
- **Advanced White-label Email:** Firm-branded email notifications; design presets by email type; customizable headers/footers; test email preview.
- **Centralized document library:** All investor documents in one place; most recent/unseen highlighted; search and filter by vehicle and doc type; batch download with folder structure.
- **Inbox:** All GP communications captured alongside documents; context-linked to tasks and shared files.
- **Tasks:** Auto-created on capital calls and other time-sensitive events; deadlines with automated reminders; multi-party collaboration tracking.
- **Profiles:** Investors view/update contact matrix and wire instructions; update requests routed to GP for review/approval before changes take effect.
- **Financial Reporting:** Three-layer modular framework (see below).

### Financial Reporting (3-Layer Framework)
1. **Fund-defined datasets:** GP defines data structure (Investments, Transactions, Investing Entities, Fund Vehicles); chooses columns; row-level permissions inherited from contact matrix; integrates via API.
2. **Flexible display widgets:** Tables, bar charts, pie charts, number cards — present same dataset multiple ways from a growing widget library.
3. **Audience-specific pages:** Multiple reporting pages per Portal instance; each page shows specific investors the data they're entitled to; supports LP overview, co-investor, and transaction-level views.
- Data can be downloaded directly by investors; optional API access for automated LP-side data feeds.
- Capital account details + transaction details live as of March 2026 (Q1 milestone).

### GP / Fund Manager Experience
- **Self-service page editor:** Web-page editor; swap section templates; upload images/videos; publish changes instantly.
- **Contact management:** Granular contact matrices at investment level; batch updates (all contacts of an entity across funds; single contact across all entities/funds; all contacts for a comm type across entities in a fund); self-service contact matrix view for investors.
- **Document distribution:** Three methods — Group Share (all/specific contacts, fund-level), Private Share (AI maps file to correct IE), Split & Share (one PDF → split by markers/page count → AI maps to IEs). AI-powered doc-type detection from filename and content.
- **Integration Hub connectivity:** No-code integrations connect Portal, FundSub, Data Rooms with CRM and other systems.

## 2026 Roadmap

- **Q2:** Reporting — Transactions & capital movements; Reporting — Portfolio & performance metrics
- **Q3:** Documents & Engagements analytics; Profiles sharing for contact & wire info updates
