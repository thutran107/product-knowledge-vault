## Summary
Core FundSub sales deck for the "Anduin Essentials" tier, targeting first-time and emerging fund managers. Designated onboarding-required for Sales + RM new hires. Defines the Essentials packaging (Starter vs Advanced), compares Essentials vs Enterprise, includes company proof points and two customer quotes.

## Key Takeaways
- **Target market:** First-time and emerging fund managers (cost-effective entry point, not large multi-fund enterprises).
- **Essentials vs Enterprise:**
  - Essentials: single fund/multiple flows OR multiple funds/single flow; max 2 investor groups (Individual and/or Entity); basic AML/KYC workflow.
  - Enterprise: multiple funds + multiple flows; more than 2 groups; customized flows; complex AML/KYC.
- **Fund structures supported under Essentials:** Domestic/Offshore, Open/Close-ended, Single or Multiple funds (with one flow), Parallel/SPV/Hybrid.
- **Two Essentials tiers:**
  - **Starter:** 18 months platform access, Marketing Data Room, digitization of 1 main sub doc.
  - **Advanced:** Starter + additional workflows (MFN, SPV, Co-invest, etc.).
- **Key features included:** Form Logic (field validation, investor-type smart logic, auto-listed AML/KYC docs), Dashboard View & Reporting, Review & Commenting, Batch Actions (countersigning), Data Import/Export, Data Room (up to 4 user groups with admin rights).
- **Other Anduin modules** (IDM, Investor Portal, Investor Access, Side Letter, IH) are unlockable at the Enterprise tier, not included in Essentials.
- **Company stats (as of Jun 2025):** Founded 2014, 140 employees, 77% dedicated to R&D, clients in North America, Europe, Asia.
- **Customer quotes:**
  - Chris Earthman (MD & Co-Founder, NXSTEP): "What took me 30–45 minutes with manual processes took 10 minutes with Anduin."
  - Lawrence Wu (Principal, Recharge Capital): praises Anduin team responsiveness as a key retention factor.
