## Summary
Internal Boston Popup deck (May 14 2025) covering Investor Portal pricing structure, competitive benchmarking, bundle recommendations, and beta prospect proposals. Most authoritative pricing source for Investor Portal as of its date.

## Key Takeaways
- Pricing is per-fund + per-SPV annually, tiered by AUM; one-time data migration fee on top.
- Six AUM tiers: Emerging ($0–250M), Established ($250M–1B), Enterprise ($1B–15B), Baby Whale ($15–50B), Adult Whale ($50–100B), Super Whale ($100B+).
- Beta discount: 50% off list price in Y1, 25% off in Y2, no discount Y3+.
- Recommended to sell as a full bundle with IDM + Landing Pages + Integration Hub.
- Portal-only buyers get Standard IDM Profile + Custom Contact Matrix + API, but not Custom IDM, KYC/AML, or Branded Landing Pages for marketing.

## Bundling: What Portal-Only Includes vs. Full Bundle

**Included with Portal (no IDM add-on):** Standard IDM Profile, Custom Contact Matrix, API
**NOT included without IDM:** Custom IDM profile, KYC/AML management

**Included with Portal (no Landing Pages add-on):** Custom homepage for each Portal instance
**NOT included without Landing Pages:** Branded Landing Pages for marketing new funds / investment opportunities

**Full bundle recommendation:** Portal + IDM + Landing Pages + Integration Hub gives customers out-of-the-box integrations, Custom IDM Profile per entity, KYC/AML, and full branding/marketing capabilities.

**If customer already has FundSub + IDM:** Keep their existing FundSub/IDM pricing as-is; add Portal fees on top.

## Competitive Pricing Intelligence

| Vendor | Pricing Structure | Level |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| AtomInvest | Per-fund, tiered by AUM | $10K/fund/yr ($1B+ AUM); $6K/fund/yr (<$1B) |
| InvestorFlow | AUM-based | $20–30K/yr ($1B AUM); $100–150K/yr ($10B AUM) |
| Intralinks | # users + document storage volume | More expensive and less predictable |
| Allvue | # LPs (tied to GL system pricing) | $60K/yr (larger); $24K/yr (smaller) |
| General market | Per-fund, sometimes # LPs | $100–200K/yr for $1B AUM per Lionpoint; $100–200K/yr FundSub+Portal bundle for $1B AUM per Holland Mountain |

**Anduin positioning:** More economical and predictable than Intralinks; competitive vs. InvestorFlow for Enterprise/Whale; stronger product bundle than pure-play accounting vendors.
