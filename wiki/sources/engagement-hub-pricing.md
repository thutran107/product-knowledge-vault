## Summary
The pricing authority for Engagement Hub. Defines a three-tier annual subscription metered by the number of *published* landing pages (Starter / Growth / Unlimited), launched with a 30% beta discount, and explains how EH restructures Investor Portal pricing. Most authoritative EH commercial source as of 2025-09-18.

## Key Takeaways
- **Pricing model:** annual fee, **3 tiers by number of published landing pages** — Starter (≤5), Growth (≤10), Unlimited.
- **30% beta discount** at launch; full pricing expected late 2026 / early 2027 as more engagement functionality ships.
- **What's included:** Branded Landing Pages + engagement functionality (Inbox, Document sharing, Profile Sharing). Future: news feed & engagement analytics (TBD).
- **Standalone landing pages:** can be sold as a standalone SKU but not recommended; offer a first-year discount, with a year-2 price increase once value is proven.
- **Published vs. draft:** only *published* pages (visible to LPs) count toward tier limits; drafts are free; pages can be unpublished/retired to fall back under the limit; usage is monitored in Anduin.
- **Exceeding the limit:** publishing continues uninterrupted; an automated alert tells RM/CS to upgrade the client to the next tier; no single add-on pages are sold.
- **Portal relationship:** Portal buyers get the **Ultimate tier** of EH by default; new Portal pricing = annual EH fee + (lower) annual per-fund Portal fee.
