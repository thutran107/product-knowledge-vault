## Summary
Internal GTM primer explaining investor portal market context, portal categories and competitive landscape, why funds adopt portals, Anduin's positioning, and acquisition targets for 2025–2026. Companion to the main sales deck; focused on market education rather than feature detail.

## Key Takeaways
- Investor portals exist because fund managers have reporting obligations (capital calls, K-1s, distributions, NAV, performance) and need a secure, branded, auditable channel.
- Five main categories of portals — Anduin sits in the "Standalone" category alongside InvestorFlow, Fundwave, and AtomInvest.
- Anduin differentiates on: end-to-end platform (onboarding + portal + data room + IDM), UX/branding, and deep workflow integration.
- Acquisition targets: $14M CFP by end of 2026 (1–2 customers/quarter in 2025, 2–3 in 2026); $20M total by end of 2026 (3–5 customers/quarter in 2025, 15–20 in 2026).

## Why Funds Adopt Portals
1. **Reporting duties** — distributing capital calls, K-1s, quarterly reports, distribution notices, NAV, capital account balances, transaction history.
2. **Operational efficiency** — batch document distribution to right stakeholders in minutes; reduce admin burden.
3. **Investor self-service** — all information in one place; standardized access regardless of when investor checks.
4. **Security** — files protected behind login; audit trails; more secure than email.
5. **Branding** — showcase firm sophistication; instill confidence in LPs.

## Portal Category Map

| Category | Examples | Anduin Strength vs. Them |
|----------|---------|--------------------------|
| Data Room-Based | Intralinks, Datasite, Merrill | Anduin has better customization and workflow depth |
| Fund Admin-Provided | SS&C, SEI, Ultimus | Anduin is purpose-built; not bundled with admin services |
| Accounting-Based | Allvue, FIS/Investran, eFront | Anduin more user-friendly; less rigid schema |
| Standalone | InvestorFlow, Fundwave, AtomInvest | Direct competitors — Anduin wins on end-to-end platform |
| Custom-Built | Proprietary | Anduin offers comparable customization without dev costs |
| Banking/Enterprise | Blackstone iLevel, BNY Mellon NEXEN | Anduin purpose-built for private markets; faster |

## Anduin Ecosystem Positioning
Portal launch redefines Anduin from a fund onboarding tool to a full investor relationship platform:
- Fund Marketing → Landing Pages
- Fundraising → Data Room + FundSub
- Post-close → Portal (documents, inbox, reporting, tasks, profiles)
- Data backbone → IDM + Integration Hub + Whitelabeling/SSO
