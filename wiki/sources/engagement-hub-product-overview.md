## Summary
The authoritative capability deck for Anduin's Engagement Hub — an IDM-based investor engagement layer that gives LPs one branded login spanning fund marketing, document distribution, communications, and profile management. Defines EH's four core features, the EH/Portal/IDM feature matrix, and the bundling/pricing strategy that restructures Investor Portal pricing. Most authoritative source on EH capability as of its last revision (Feb 2026).

## Key Takeaways
- **EH = four core features:** Branded Landing Pages, Document Distribution, Communication (centralized inbox), Profile Sharing — all built on the IDM Firm space.
- **One URL / one login** for LPs: vehicle info, incoming opportunities, marketing data room, subscription workflows, confidential fund updates, reports & newsletters.
- **Landing pages** are self-service (web builder) with granular access controls; use cases include early fund marketing (IOIs), co-branded/advisor pages, co-investments, and advisory committee engagement.
- **Feature matrix:** IDM-only (data + AML/KYC), Engagement Hub (landing pages + communication + profile sharing + document distribution), Investor Portal (= EH **plus** Portal instance & Reporting). All three packages use the same IDM Firm space.
- **Enablement (internal):** Production via iTools > IDM > Portal Package.
- **Bundling strategy:** lead with bundles by default; raise IDM pricing; lower EH pricing to drive early adoption; bundle EH with IDM. Restructures Portal pricing to "annual EH fee + (lower) annual per-fund Portal fee."
- **Reporting Dashboards are Portal-only** (explicitly marked as Investor Portal package, not EH).
