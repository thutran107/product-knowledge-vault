## Summary
The official customer-facing marketing one-pager for Engagement Hub. Positions EH as a branded, invite-only space to "own the investor experience, from first touchpoint to post-close," fixing the fragmented LP journey (multiple logins, insecure email, relationships going cold after fundraising). Narrow scope — marketing positioning only; capability and pricing detail live in higher-trust sources.

## Key Takeaways
- **Positioning line:** "Own the investor experience, from first touchpoint to post-close."
- **Problem framing:** LPs juggle multiple apps/URLs/logins; GP-LP comms are unsecured/fragmented email; post-fundraising relationships go cold.
- **EH value props:** cohesive fully-branded journey; dedicated landing pages for upcoming opportunities; secure document sharing (individual or group); LP self-management of Anduin profiles; content/fund visibility tailored by investor group (Institutional / Retail / RIA); direct LP communication (newsletters, announcements, updates); one secure centralized hub.
- **Outcomes pitched:** boost LP engagement and conversion; strengthen relationships; no coding required; purpose-built for private markets.
