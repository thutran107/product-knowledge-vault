## Summary
Describes the Integration Hub's purpose — a centralized self-service platform for prospects and customers to explore, configure, and activate integrations without extensive technical support. Covers the five core structural components of the Hub.

## Key Takeaways
- The Hub exists to shorten time-to-value by replacing custom demos and heavy technical pre-sales with self-service configuration.
- Access requires being authenticated on Anduin AND having an email domain registered to an existing Entity.
- Five core components: Organization, Organization Email Domain, Integration Hub, Integration Card, Integration (instance), and Anduin Object.
- "Integration Card" = reusable prebuilt workflow template for a specific app/use case.
- "Integration" = an active instance of a card tied to a specific configuration.
- "Anduin Object" = a fund (FundSub), data room (Data Room), or entity (IDM) that integrations connect to.
