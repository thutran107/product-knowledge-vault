## Summary
Documents the naming and packaging history of Anduin's **Branded Landing Page** and how it became the **Engagement Hub** solution, based on internal Slack announcements from the product and marketing teams (Aug 2024 – Aug 2025). The naming facts are well-corroborated across multiple posts by the product lead (Amy Nguyen) and marketing (My Anh); treat as authoritative on *naming/packaging* but low-trust for capability/pricing detail (Slack sits below the §10 hierarchy — defer to the EH spec and pricing decks for substance).

## Key Takeaways
- **"Landing page" = "Branded Landing Page" = the same product** — "landing page" is shorthand. Sales/CS explicitly avoided presenting them as different products.
- **Original internal name: "IDM Branding."** The feature was introduced under that working name in Oct 2024 before "Branded Landing Page" was adopted.
- **Repackaged under Engagement Hub in Aug 2025.** Marketing first *proposed renaming* Branded Landing Page → "Engagement Hub" outright (Aug 6); the final outcome was that **Branded Landing Page became one of four features within the Engagement Hub solution**, not a straight rename.
- **Engagement Hub = 4 features** at launch: Branded Landing Page, Inbox, Document Sharing, Profile Sharing.
- **Public launch: Aug 27, 2025** — website page, PR Newswire article, one-pager, social.
- **Disambiguation rule:** when someone says "landing page," they usually mean the Branded Landing Page *feature* specifically — not the full Engagement Hub bundle.
