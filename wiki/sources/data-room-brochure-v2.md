## Summary
External-facing brochure for Anduin Data Room as of March 2024. Positions Data Room as a simple, secure, custom-branded investor document sharing platform with real-time engagement analytics and one-click Fund Subscription handoff. Includes a customer quote from Accolade Partners.

## Key Takeaways
- **Scale proof point (2024):** ~500k documents uploaded, 100k+ monthly views.
- **Granular permissions:** four levels — Viewing, Downloading, Editing, Managing — set per document and investor group.
- **Custom branding:** drag-and-drop homepage editor, custom URL with white-label option, videos and images supported.
- **Investor activity insights:** real-time tracking of views, visits, time spent, downloads; identify most engaging documents.
- **Admin efficiency:** batch invitations and reminders with customizable email templates; comprehensive activity log.
- **FundSub integration:** one-click transition from Data Room to Fund Subscription — called out explicitly as a key differentiator.
- **Pricing positioning:** "straightforward pricing — fixed annual fee"; "unlimited data rooms with no time limits."
- **Security & compliance:** top-tier security and global compliance named as a key value prop.
- **Customer quote:** Andrew Salembier (Partner & COO, Accolade Partners) — singles out multi-group access levels and the Data Room → FundSub one-click flow as unique differentiators.
